# Estee Bedding – Return policy

## General

Customers are responsible for **all return shipping.** Any products returned must be in a **sealed corrugated carton**, and the customer is responsible for the shipping costs.

**Return address:**
Estee Bedding Company  
945 E 93rd Street  
Chicago, IL 60619  
Attn: Returns  

## 100 Nights' Free Sleep

We're so confident that you will love your Estee Bedding mattress that we offer a **100 Nights' Free Sleep** policy. If for any reason you are not happy with your mattress, you may return it for a refund within **100 days of purchase.** Please call our Customer Service Department at **1-800-521-REST (7378)** to request a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA).

## Wrong item or size

If you ordered the wrong item or size, call our Customer Service Department at **1-800-521-REST (7378)**, and we'll take care of you. Please note that a **20% restocking fee** will be applied for the return of any clean, unused product that was purchased in error.

## Return merchandise credit

Return merchandise credit will only be given for products that are **not damaged** when received at Estee Bedding Company.

## Warranty service

If you think your mattress or any other item may be **defective**, please visit our Warranty page (do not use the general return policy for warranty claims).

## Checking on a return

If you have any questions about a returned item, call our Customer Service team at **1-800-521-REST (7378)**.
