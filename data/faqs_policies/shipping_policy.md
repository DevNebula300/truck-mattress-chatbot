# Estee Bedding – Shipping policy

We offer **FREE shipping** on all our products via **UPS** anywhere in the **continental United States.** Please note that UPS does not ship to P.O. Boxes or APO/FPO addresses.

Your plush Made to Ride mattress is **made to order** by skilled **Union workers** in our **Chicago factory.**

## Timing

- Your order will **ship within 5–7 business days** after the order is received.
- Once an order is in transit, **shipping time is typically 2–5 business days**, depending on the destination.

So if a customer asks "how long to get to LA if I ordered today?" — the order typically ships within 5–7 business days; after that, delivery to the West Coast (e.g. LA) is usually within about 2–5 business days. They will receive tracking once the order ships.

## Packaging

Our mattresses ship **folded, rolled up, and vacuum sealed.** This packaging process allows us to ship safely and efficiently.

## Large or special orders

If an order exceeds the size and weight guidelines of UPS, or if the order cannot ship via UPS, we will contact you.
