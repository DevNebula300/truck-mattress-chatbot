# Estee Bedding – Warranty

All Estee Bedding mattresses are covered by a **1 YEAR LIMITED WARRANTY** against defects in materials or workmanship. This warranty extends only to the **original purchaser.**

## Warranty service

For warranty service, the customer is responsible for **returning the item** in a **sealed corrugated carton** sufficient to guard against damage. We will ship the customer a new mattress, provided that the mattress meets our warranty requirements. **Shipping cost** for the replacement will be the **responsibility of the customer.**

## Normal wear (not covered)

It is **normal for body impressions** to appear on a mattress with regular use. Such impressions are **not structural defects** covered by this warranty. They are simply an indication that the upholstery layers are settling and compacting as you first use your new mattress.

## What the warranty does not cover

- Damage in shipping or installation  
- Normal wear and tear (e.g. discoloration of cover or components, foam compression or cover quilt compression)  
- Damage from stains or soiling  
- Damage from tampering or opening the product  
- Damage from misuse and/or abuse  
- Damage from laundering, dry-cleaning, or chemical solutions  

## Warranty is void if

- Item has been damaged due to tampering or opening  
- Item has been damaged due to misuse or abuse  
- Item has been sold or transferred to a secondary user (warranty valid to original purchaser only)  

It is Estee Bedding's sole discretion to refuse a warranty claim based upon the condition of the item when returned.

## Scope

Warranty is valid **only within the United States** and is **non-transferrable.** For full details, customers should visit our Warranty page.
