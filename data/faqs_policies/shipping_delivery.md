# Shipping and delivery (quick reference)

Use this together with the full **shipping policy** when customers ask how long delivery takes or shipping to a specific place (e.g. LA).

- **Free shipping** via UPS in the continental United States (no P.O. Box or APO/FPO).
- Orders **ship within 5–7 business days** after the order is received.
- Once in transit, delivery is typically **2–5 business days** depending on destination (e.g. LA / West Coast is in that range).
- Mattresses are made to order in our Chicago factory and ship folded, rolled, and vacuum sealed. Customers receive tracking once the order ships.

So for *"How long to get to LA if I ordered today?"* — ship within 5–7 business days, then about 2–5 business days in transit to the West Coast; they'll get tracking when it ships.
