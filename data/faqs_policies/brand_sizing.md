# Estee Bedding – brand and sizing

## Sizing: we use dimensions, not mattress “names”

- We **do not sell by names** like "Short Queen," "Twin XL," "Queen," or "King."
- We sell by **exact dimensions**: **length x width x thickness** in inches (e.g. **36 x 79 x 7″**, **42 x 80 x 8.5″**).
- When a customer says they have a "short queen" or "twin XL" bunk, help them by matching to our **dimension sizes** from the mattress catalog (e.g. 36 x 75″, 36 x 79″, 42 x 78″, 42 x 80″) and say we sell by dimensions — the size that fits would be [give the dimension]. Suggest they use our Size Finder or measure their bunk for the best fit.

## Made in the USA

- All Estee Bedding truck mattresses are **made in the USA**. You can say this when customers ask about origin or quality.
